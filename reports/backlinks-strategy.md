## Outreach Email Templates

### Template 1: Directory Submission
```
Subject: Utilyx — 50+ Free Online Tools (100% Private, No Signup)

Hi,

I'd like to submit Utilyx (https://utilyx.app) to your directory.

Utilyx is a free multi-tool suite offering 50+ online tools:
- PDF: compress, merge, convert, sign, unlock
- Images: resize, compress, convert, background removal
- Video: trim, compress, convert, extract audio
- Dev/SEO: meta tags, JSON formatter, regex tester
- Generators: QR codes, passwords, UUIDs, WhatsApp links
- Calculators: BMI, concrete, mileage

Key differentiator: 100% local browser processing — no data ever 
leaves the user's device. No signup required.

Available in 6 languages: FR, EN, ES, DE, AR, PT

Would you consider adding us?

Best,
[Name]
```
